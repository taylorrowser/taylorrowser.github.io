One player will recieve a bizarre piece of space junk on their screen, and they can either choose to describe it to the rest of the players, or make something up on the fly. The ultimate goal is to decieve, so if you describe the thing on your screen, you want players to think that you are talking straight out of your ass. Points will be awarded to those who trick and those who see right through the cloud of lies.
